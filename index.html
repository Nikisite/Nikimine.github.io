Minecraft

Minecraft is an indie sandbox video game created by Swedish programmer Markus Persson and published by his company Mojang AB. Persson published the initial version of the game in 2009; at the end of 2011 a stable version for PC Microsoft Windows was released and distributed through the official website . In subsequent years, Minecraft was ported to Linux and macOS for personal computers; on Android, iOS and Windows Phone for mobile devices; game consoles PlayStation 4, Vita, VR, Xbox One, Nintendo 3DS , Switch and Wii U. In 2014, Microsoft Corporation acquired the rights to Minecraft with Mojang AB for 2.5 billion $. Studio 4J ported the game to consoles, while Xbox Game Studios developed a multi-platform version of Minecraft and a special edition of the game for educational institutions.

Critics considered the presence of unfinished items and difficulties with the creation of a network game, as well as the absence of a training mode in the game, to be considered shortcomings. The developers have eliminated these shortcomings in subsequent years. Interesting facts

  1. Initially Creeper was not a mob - it appeared as a result of an error when creating a pig. Later, he was retextured and added the ability to explode.
  2. The snow golem has a face behind a pumpkin mask.
  3. With a 0.01% chance in the main menu, instead of a large inscription "Minecraft", the name with the error "Minceraft" will appear.
  4. The language of the Wanderers of the Edge (Endermen) is English backwards.
  5. The workbench in the Minecraft world can be used as a compass - on its northern part there will always be a texture with a saw and a hammer, regardless of the position of the player at the time the block is placed.
  6. All cows in Minecraft are female, as they all give milk.
  7. A pig can be turned into a zombie pig when struck by lightning.
  8. And if you hit the Creeper with lightning, it will become charged. The strength and radius of the explosion will be increased.
  9. The Enderman cannot be hit by an arrow from a bow, a snowball, or an egg. You can't drop an anvil on it either.
  10. However, if you shoot him with an arrow with an effect, then even though the arrow does not hit the mob, he will still receive its effect.
  11. The chest cannot be set on fire or moved by a piston.
  12. Ifrit caught in the web cannot shoot. He can also be hit with snowballs.
  13. The spawning of Slugs depends on the phase of the moon - most of them will be on the full moon, and on the new moon the spawning of Slugs is suspended.
  14. The hieroglyphs in the enchantment table are not spontaneously drawn letters, but a real cipher that makes sense.
  15. Creepers, although they do not have arms, are still able to climb stairs.
  1. Never sleep in the Lower World. When interacting with the bed, it will explode and you will receive damage.
  2. A cactus in the house can be used instead of a trash can - when a dropped item touches it, it disappears.
  3. Renaming a mob with a tag using the names "Grumm" or "Dinnerbone" will flip that mob's game model.
  4. If you rename a large or medium slug, then after killing it, smaller slugs will also keep the name given to the "ancestor".
  5. The most interesting thing about the tags: if you rename the sheep to "jeb_", then it will become iridescent. Jeb_ is the nickname of one of the developers of the game.
  6. A little about the developers: Notch and Jeb_ (Markus Persson and Jens Bergensten, respectively) were included in the 100 most influential people in the world in 2013 according to Time magazine.
  7. If a chicken/pig/cow/sheep are killed while being set on fire, the meat that falls from them will be immediately fried.
  8. The concept for the black and white cat in Minecraft was Jens's real cat, Newton.
  9. The smallest mobs in the game are the turtle baby, the Ender silverfish and the bat.
  10. If you die in a battle with Gast, or be in his field of vision at the time of death, then he will still shoot at you until you respawn.
  11. Putting a pumpkin on your head, you can easily look at the Wanderers of the Land - they will not attack you.
  12. Skeletons usually have a bow in the right hand, but with a 5% chance it can appear in the left.
  13. Endermen take water damage. Moreover, they can be damaged with a water bottle.
  14. Snowballs can repel Ghast fireballs.
  15. And on December 24-25, the chests take on a festive Christmas look.

Commands for single play

# Command Function
1 /gamemode 0, 1, 2 Change game mode: Survival, Creative, Hardcore
2 /time set, add [number] Changes the game time (0 - dawn, 6000 - noon, 12000 - sunset, 18000 - midnight).
3 /xp [number] Adds as much health as the player specifies
4 /kill Character instantly dies
5 /give [item id] [amount] Gives out blocks in the specified number
6 /effect [player name] [effect] [duration] [level] Places an effect on the selected player with the specified level and duration. All numbers must be written in brackets.
7 /spawnpoint [player name] [x] [y] [z] Sets the spawn point for the player. If you enter the coordinates, then the hero will respawn exactly according to them. If this is not done, the spawn point will be set to where you are currently standing.
8 /toggledownfal Changes the weather to snowy or rainy. Precipitation is turned off by the same command.
9 /tp [player 1] [player 2] Teleports one player to another. You will need to write the names of the players in brackets.
10 /publish Opens the world to all users who are connected to your PC via LAN or Hamachi.
11 /enchant [player name] [effect] [level] Places an effect on the selected player with the specified level and duration. All numbers must be written in brackets.
12 /give Player minecraft:[subject] [number] Gives out [number] [subject]

Commands for online play

# Command Function
1 /say [text] A message that all players on the server will see
2 /tell [player] [text] The message that the selected player will see
3 /list Returns a list of all players on the server
4 /op [player] The selected player becomes an administrator
5 /deop [player] Selected player ceases to be an administrator
6 /ban [player] The selected player is banned
7 /ban-ip [ip address] Selected player gets banned by IP address
8 /banlist Returns a list of all banned players
9 /sorry [player] Removed the ban from the player
10 /pardon-ip [ip-address] Remove ban from IP address
11 /kick [player] The player is permanently disconnected from the server
12 /whitelist add [player] Player is whitelisted
13 /white list Returns a list of all players that are on the whitelist
14 /whitelist on Enable whitelisting on the server
15 /whitelist off Disable whitelisting on the server
16 /whitelist reload Manual whitelist update
17 /remove [player] The selected player is removed from the whitelist
18 /save-all Save map changes
19 /save-off Disable auto save world
20 /save-on Enable automatic world saving
21 /stop Disable server
